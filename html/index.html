Miranda Card

  1. 1. You have the right to remain silent.
  2. 2. Anything you say can and will be used against you in a court of law.
  3. 3. You have the right to talk to a lawyer and have him present
    with you at any time during questioning.
  4. 4. If you cannot afford a lawyer one will be appointed to you by
    the state, prior to you being questioned.
  5. 5. You can decide at any time to exercise these rights and not answer any
    questions, or make any statements.

Waiver

Do you understand each of these rights I have explained to you?

Having these rights in mind, would you still wish to speak to me?
